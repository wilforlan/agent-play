# Code of Conduct

We want collaboration to be respectful and productive.

## Our standards

- **Be respectful** — Critique ideas, not people. Assume good intent.
- **Be constructive** — Share feedback that helps improve the work or the discussion.
- **Be inclusive** — Welcome newcomers; avoid jargon without explanation when it helps others follow along.
- **Stay on topic** — Keep issues and PRs focused on the project.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insulting comments, or sustained disruption.
- Sharing private information of others without consent.

## Reporting

If you see behavior that violates this code, report it to the maintainers with a clear description of what happened and where (issue, PR, or channel).

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove or edit content, or temporarily or permanently exclude participants who violate these rules, in line with platform policies.

This document is a lightweight baseline; projects may adopt stricter community guidelines over time by mutual agreement among contributors.
